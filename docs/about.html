Background

  • Research shows people deliberately subject themselves to negative stimuli, suggesting a behavioral preference for such stimuli Kerr, Siegle, and Orsini (2019); Oosterwijk (2017)

  • People rated high in morbid curiosity tend to identity with the "horror fandom" Scrivner and Christensen (2021); Scrivner (2021)

  • Research indicates individuals use horror content to cope with anxiety & regulate emotions ("Self-Trigger") Scrivner and Christensen (2021); Bellet, Jones, and McNally (2020)

  • Horror fan motivations characterized by the Horror Typology Scale Scrivner et al. (2022)
